This folder contains the core principles and philosophies that I follow.

These notes represent the *why* behind the projects and materials found elsewhere on this site. Note that they are, for the most part, living documents that I regularly test and update through my own learning. I have included references where appropriate.

Before exploring other parts of this site, I invite you to take a look through these philosophies to help you understand my commitment to open access to information, learner autonomy, and universal design.

> [!info] I'm open to discussion!
> I welcome different perspectives that challenge or expand on the ideas I have written about here. I’m particularly interested in how these philosophies interact with real-world practice in accessibility and information design.
>
